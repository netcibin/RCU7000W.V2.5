Info

RCU-7000W, the cutting-edge revolutionizing hospitality management Room Control Unit.

Powered by an Xtensa dual-core MCU, this sleek device boasts 520 KiB of RAM and 448 KiB of ROM, ensuring swift and efficient operation. Equipped with 802.11 b/g/n Wi-Fi and Bluetooth v4.2, it seamlessly integrates into any network environment, offering both BR/EDR and BLE capabilities. With 2 UART pins and RS485 Full Duplex Modbus support, RCU-7000W facilitates seamless communication with a variety of devices. Its 5 DI inputs and 7 DO outputs provide extensive control options, while the inclusion of 1 SPDT relay output and 13 DTDP relay outputs enhances versatility and functionality. Tailored for King Size Hotel Rooms, RCU-7000W combines advanced technology with user-friendly design to streamline room management and enhance guest experience.
